The asset names match what `.github/workflows/app-release.yml` uploads
(`rgb-sunglasses-<version>.apk` and `rgb-sunglasses-<version>-qr.png`).

![Download QR code](https://github.com/skalldri/rgb-sunglasses/releases/download/app-v<version>/rgb-sunglasses-<version>-qr.png)
